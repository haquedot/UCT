About Studio

Where creativity meets technology

We believe that your success is our success and hence, we strive to provide you with a comprehensive business solution model where we ideate, create, market, and deliver your success at your doorstep.

Ulta Cube Technologies, which began as an Animation service company, has earned a reputable name for itself in the field of advertising, riding high on the never-fading trust of its 70+ clients. Our unparalleled dedication to utmost customer satisfaction has catapulted us to a league of our own that speaks volumes about our positive outlook on the overall customer experience.

Our team of young innovators has converged to build a groundbreaking platform that fulfils all your needs- from ideation to creation, and from branding to marketing. We are fueled by our commitment against substandard content, sharing in the quiet rebellion that inspires those who do not intend to compromise on the quality of digital media the world should consume.

Ulta Cube Technologies, which began as an animation service company, has earned a reputable name for itself in the field of advertising, marketing, and branding, backed by the trust of 70+ global clients. Our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction has placed us in a league of our own, showcasing our positive approach to the customer experience. Ulta Cube is a one-stop studio for your digital needs as we excel in brand building through Logos, Brochures, Website Designing, Company Portfolios, Business Presentations, Explainer Videos, Product Videos, Whiteboard Animation, Corporate Videos, as well as Corporate Event Management and Digital Marketing services.

Registered with the Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi, Ulta Cube Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a leading name in the field of Advertisement, Branding, and Digital Marketing, guided by accomplished and renowned Digital Media technocrats.

Vision Statement:
To be the most trusted partner in the domain of digital media by providing unique end-to-end business solutions, delivering excellent products, and creating an impact that brings lasting benefits to our customers and the communities in which we live and work.

Mission Statement:
Our mission is to be an impactful leader that drives and transforms the lives of individuals and communities by maintaining the highest integrity with our employees and our esteemed clients through our world-class business services and maintaining the best-quality deliverables.

Quality Policy:

  • Quality is a way of life for the employees and management team of Ulta Cube Technologies.
  • We strive hard to deliver error-free services the first time and every time the customer approaches us for various products and services.
  • We will work closely with our customers to fully understand their needs and meet their expectations throughout the life of their contract with us.
  • We will make sure our employees are trained as per the latest trends in the industry and that they have the skills, knowledge, and experience to perform their roles effectively.
  • We will review the suitability and effectiveness of our systems and processes at regular intervals to meet the requirements of our esteemed clients.
